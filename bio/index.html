Food: I am omnivorous. I enjoy cooking dinner a lot, and would even be glad to cook for roommates a few times a week, assuming you can stand my cuisine. I really love barbecue grilling, if there’s outdoor space. I like having a clean and tidy kitchen.

Shared space: I have no pets and don’t smoke. I don’t own a lot of furniture I plan to keep. I like to keep common spaces tidy and clutter-free. Social life: If desired, I will always try to offer advance notice and get consent before having guests over, which isn’t super often. I enjoy hosting dinner parties or board game nights periodically. I do like having big house parties or barbecues every once in a while. I would like to have the option of having out-of-town guests visit for a few days, but again only with notice and your consent. Noise level: I enjoy listening to music and playing acoustic guitar, but I am happy to negotiate appropriate volumes and times of day! 

Style: I do my best to be a respectful housemate and to be mindful of the needs and interests of my neighbors. I am pretty easy-going about most things home-related. I try really hard to be self-aware and responsible to the people around me. I strive for clear, compassionate, and supportive communication with my housemates. After all, we’re sharing a home, right?

What I'm looking for: A bedroom with high ceilings and a lot of light, within a couple miles of the Mission District. A kitchen with a dishwasher and laundry facilities on the premises. Friendly, responsible housemates. Some reasonable place to store my camping gear. The rest is negotiable. :)
